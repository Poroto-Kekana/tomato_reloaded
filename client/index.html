We are a team centred around the love of agriculture and precision farming. Our work also contributes to reducing economic loss caused by crop losses and lower yields caused by diseases.


Our aim is to make a significant contribution towards Sustainable Agricultural Development. ‘As the world continues to grow ,much more effort and innovation will be urgently needed in order to increase agricultural production, improve the global supply chain, decrease food losses and waste, and ensure that all who are suffering from hunger and malnutrition have access to nutritious food’.
