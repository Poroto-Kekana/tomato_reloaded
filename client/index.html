WHO WE ARE

Creating a better place for farmers

We are a team centred around the love of agriculture and 4IR inspired. Our main goal is to provide farmers with an innovative system that will help them to diagnose crop disease within a blink of an eye, thus creating a better world for them.


We are passionate about food security and United Nation SDG goals

We aim in helping to accomplish the Agricultural Sustainable Development goal which is to End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture. The sustainable development goal clearly states that ‘As the world continues to grow ,much more effort and innovation will be urgently needed in order to increase agricultural production, improve the global supply chain, decrease food losses and waste, and ensure that all who are suffering from hunger and malnutrition have access to nutritious food ’. This is where we come in, as a team we aim to help achieve these goals by developing a webApp that will help the farmers to detect tomato leaf diseases at an early stage. This will help to reduce food loss caused by diseases as the farmers can quickly treat the detected diseases.
